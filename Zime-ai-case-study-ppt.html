15 hours/week of manual reviews

Can't observe 100–200 calls/week

No objective playbook measurement

Success Metrics & Validation Plan

⏳ Pending baseline
📈 Deal Velocity Improvement
Formula
Median(Days to Close) - Control Group vs Pilot Group
How measured: Track median sales cycle for pilot reps (using dashboard) vs control reps (not using). Baseline from CRM. Target: 15% faster (42 days → 36 days).
⏳ Pending baseline
🎯 Win Rate Lift
Formula
(Pilot Win% - Control Win%) in percentage points
How measured: Compare win rates (deals won / deals closed) for pilot vs control. Baseline: 36%. Target: +3pp lift (36% → 39%). Dollar impact: ~$150K ARR.
⏳ Pending baseline
✅ Playbook Adoption Rate
Formula
Σ(Actions Completed) / Σ(Actions Expected) × 100
How measured: Track completion of playbook actions (budget qualification, multi-threading, etc.) via call transcripts. Baseline: 65%. Target: 70% completion.

Each metric has clear formula, measurement method, and threshold. No ambiguity on what "success" means.

⏱ Baseline Collection

🎯 Acceptance Gates

Must hit 1 of 3 core metrics (time/velocity/win rate) + 70% adoption + 9/10 NPS to proceed to full rollout.
